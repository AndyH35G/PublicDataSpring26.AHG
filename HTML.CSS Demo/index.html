This is a short review on the bag I use primarily for card games, The bag is the "UNIQLO Multi Pocket Shoulder Bag." It really is well to hold everything I bring such deck boxes, dices, and binder. There is only one issue which is fitting a mat. I use a mat tube so my mat doesn't get dirty or wrinkled and is a struggle to put in the bag most of the time but the bag does get the job done. I do recommend this bag to people that may need a new bag for travel or for hobbies.
